About

    I care about sustainability and societal equality. I have a PhD (2012) and Masters (2008) from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and a Bachelors (2006) from University College Dublin.

    I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the National GeoComputation Center (Ireland) where I am working with Alexei Pozdnoukhov as part of the StratAG project. In 2012, I co-founded urbmet.com with Daniel Wiesmann. We make geospatial tools to facilitate analysis and have clients in the US and Europe.
